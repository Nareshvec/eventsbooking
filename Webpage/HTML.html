Our Services

Photography requires a specific set of equipment to capture the special moments of a couple's big day. Here's a list of essential wedding photography equipment:

Camera Bodies:

  • 2 or more professional-grade DSLR or mirrorless camera bodies for backup.
  • Popular choices include Canon EOS 5D series, Nikon D800/D850, Sony Alpha series.

Lenses:

  • Wide-Angle Lens (e.g., 24mm or 35mm) for capturing venue and group shots.
  • Standard Zoom Lens (e.g., 24-70mm f/2.8) for versatile coverage.
  • Telephoto Lens (e.g., 70-200mm f/2.8) for candid shots and ceremony details.
  • Fast Prime Lens (e.g., 50mm f/1.4 or f/1.8) for low-light situations and artistic portraits.
  • Macro Lens for detailed shots of rings, flowers, etc.

External Flash Units:

  • At least two powerful external flash units for indoor or low-light situations.
  • Flash diffusers or softboxes for softer and more natural-looking light.

Tripod:

  • Sturdy tripod for group shots, low-light conditions, and stability during long exposures.

Lighting Equipment:

  • Portable LED lights or continuous lighting for creative shots.
  • Reflectors for natural light enhancement.
